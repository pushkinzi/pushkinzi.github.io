Research

I have been told that I am too young and green to have a wheelhouse. I would naively say however that my primary focuses are in algebraic geometry, vertex operator algebras, and monoidal/tensor categories.

Recent Activity (Updated Nov. 2024)

  • I recently submitted my master's thesis which you can access here. It is an expositional description of operator product expansion algberas (and so VOAs), in the language of algebraic geometry, starting from the basics of jet theory in the easy-to-visualise case of smooth manifolds.
  • Some compatriots and I run a Gromov-Witten theory reading group at the University of Melbourne which you can find here or alternatively the notes posted on this site.
  • I was recently invited to attend the Australian math conference MATRIX for the topic of 'Tensor Categories, Quantum Symmetries, and Mathematical Physics' where I was a student speaker on pseudo-tensor categories and chiral algebras.
